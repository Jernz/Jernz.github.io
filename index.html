Good News

Jesus Christ, crucified and risen

The heart of the Christian faith: God’s love revealed in Jesus, the forgiveness of sins, and new life for all who believe.

“For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whosoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life.” — John 3:16 (KJV)

The Gospel in four movements

1) Creation

God created a good world and made humanity in His image to know Him and steward creation.

2) Fall

Humanity rebelled. Sin fractured our relationship with God, one another, and creation.

3) Redemption

Jesus lived, died on the cross for our sins, and rose again so we may be reconciled to God.

4) Restoration

Jesus will return. God is making all things new. We’re invited to follow Him in hope.

Core beliefs

Scripture

The Bible is inspired by God and trustworthy for faith and practice.

God

One God in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Jesus Christ

Fully God and fully man, crucified for our sins, risen, ascended, and coming again.

Salvation

By grace through faith in Jesus, not by works, for all who repent and believe.

The Church

A community of disciples called to worship, witness, serve, and make disciples.

Life in the Spirit

The Spirit empowers holy living, spiritual gifts, and mission in the world.

Readings from the Gospels

Sermon on the Mount

“Blessed are the poor in spirit: for theirs is the kingdom of heaven.” — Matthew 5:3 (KJV)

See Matthew 5–7 for Jesus’ teaching on the kingdom, prayer, and righteousness.

The Great Commission

“Go ye therefore, and teach all nations… I am with you alway.” — Matthew 28:19–20 (KJV)

Jesus sends His disciples to make disciples of all nations.

Good Shepherd

“I am the good shepherd: the good shepherd giveth his life for the sheep.” — John 10:11 (KJV)

Jesus cares for and lays down His life for His people.

Resurrection

“He is not here: for he is risen.” — Matthew 28:6 (KJV)

The cornerstone of Christian hope.
